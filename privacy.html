your privacy matters to us

This privacy statement describes how Trueson uses your personal data

Trueson is respectful of the protection of your privacy during your use of our website. For this reason information about visitors to this website is handled with care, security and confidentiality.

Trueson uses your personal data as follows

Your personal data is stored in our database for customer relationship management and performing targeted marketing activities. These include distributing our newsletters, a request for contact or for sending information that you have requested. Trueson will not make your personal data available to third parties and will comply with the requirements of the Data Protection Act.

Cookies

Trueson uses cookies. A cookie contains information that is stored on the hard disk of your computer. Cookies do not contain personal data and they are used only to make this website easier to use and more relevant to you. You can delete these cookies from your computer.



Trueson reserves the right to change its privacy policy at all times.
